# ⚡ HA VPN: Alternative Cloud VPN Gateway

In addition to Classic VPN, Google Cloud offers **HA VPN** – a **high availability Cloud VPN** solution.

- **Purpose:** Securely connect your on-premises network to your **Virtual Private Cloud (VPC)** through an IPsec VPN connection in a single region.  
- **SLA:** 99.99% service availability ✅  

> For detailed HA VPN topologies, refer to the **Cloud VPN documentation**.  
> For guidance on migrating, see **Moving to HA VPN**.
